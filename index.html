    Brittany Carlton is currently a coding bootcamp student with the dream of becoming a frontend developer. Her passion for bringing beautiful, interactive, and responsive web apps to life grows every day! At the end of her coding bootcamp journey, she will earn a certificate from the prestigious Case Western Reserve University.

Brittany also enjoys reading, playing video and tabletop games, and dreaming of far-off lands that she hopes to visit one day.
